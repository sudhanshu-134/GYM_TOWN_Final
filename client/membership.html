Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for a membership?

You can sign up for a membership by creating an account on our website and selecting your preferred plan. After completing the payment, your membership will be activated immediately.

Can I change my membership plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your membership plan at any time through your account dashboard. The price difference will be prorated for the remaining billing period.

Is there a contract or commitment period?

Our memberships are offered on a month-to-month basis with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

Are there any additional fees?

There is a one-time registration fee of ₹25 for new members. All features mentioned in the plan descriptions are included in the monthly price with no hidden fees.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons or extended travel. A small administrative fee may apply.
